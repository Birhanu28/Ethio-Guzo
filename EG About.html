    About Ethio Guzo

    Company Overview

    Ethio Guzo is a modern and customer-focused bus transportation service operating routes between Gondar, Bahir Dar, and Addis Ababa. With a commitment to safety, comfort, and reliability, we provide high-quality intercity travel experiences for locals, tourists, and professionals alike.

    Mission

    To connect Ethiopian cities with safe, affordable, and comfortable transportation, while delivering exceptional customer service at every step of the journey.

    Vision

    To become Ethiopia’s most trusted and innovative intercity bus transportation company, setting the standard for quality, sustainability, and digital convenience.

    Our History

    Ethio Guzo was founded in 2025 in Gondar with the goal of solving intercity travel challenges by offering dependable and tech-enabled transportation solutions. What began with a single bus and a vision has now grown into a trusted name along major northern routes.

    Our Fleet

    We maintain a modern and well-serviced fleet to ensure that every journey is smooth, punctual, and safe. All our buses are equipped with:

    • Comfortable reclining seats
    • Air conditioning and ventilation
    • Onboard charging ports
    • Luggage compartments
    • GPS tracking and speed control
    • Sanitization and cleanliness protocols
