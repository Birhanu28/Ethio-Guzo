    Frequently Asked Questions

    Booking & Tickets

    1. How do I book a bus ticket on Ethio Guzo?

    Simply go to the booking page, select your origin, destination, date, and service type, then fill in your personal info and confirm your booking.

    2. Can I book a ticket without creating an account?

    Yes, guest booking is allowed, but creating an account helps you manage your tickets and view your travel history.

    3. How many passengers can I book at once?

    You can book up to 10 passengers in a single booking.

    4. Can I choose my seat while booking?

    Yes, seat selection is available during booking for most buses.

    5. Can I book a round-trip ticket?

    Yes, just check the "Return Trip" option and select your return date and time.

    6. How will I receive my ticket after booking?

    You’ll receive a digital ticket via email and SMS. You can also download it from your profile page.

    7. Can I cancel or change my booking?

    Yes, changes and cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before departure. Conditions may apply.

    8. What should I do if I miss my bus?

    Unfortunately, missed buses are non-refundable, but contact our support for help rebooking if seats are available.

    Payments & Fees

    9. What payment methods are supported?

    We accept Telebirr, HelloCash, and Cash on Bus.

    10. Is my payment secure?

    Yes, all payments are securely processed using encrypted channels.

    11. Do I need to pay online, or can I pay in cash?

    You can choose either option during checkout.

    12. What happens if my payment fails?

    If the payment fails but is deducted, contact support with your transaction ID. We’ll verify and assist you.

    13. Is there an extra fee for booking online?

    No hidden fees. The price shown is the final amount unless you select premium services.

    14. Can I book and pay later?

    No. Payment is required to confirm your seat.

    User Account

    15. How do I create an Ethio Guzo account?

    Click on the “Sign Up” button, enter your details, and verify your email.

    16. How do I reset my password?

    Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password," and follow the instructions.

    17. How can I update my email or phone number?

    After logging in, go to your profile settings and update your information.

    18. Where can I see my booking history?

    Your booking history is available under your profile dashboard once logged in.

    19. Can I use the same account to book for family members?

    Yes, just enter their names and details during the booking.

    Services & Bus Information

    20. What services do you offer on the bus?

    We offer two service types: Standard (Without Food) and Premium (With Food + More Comfort).

    21. Are meals available on board?

    Yes, for Premium service only. Light snacks and water are provided.

    22. What type of buses are in your fleet?

    Modern, air-conditioned buses with comfortable seating. Some buses have Wi-Fi and charging ports.

    23. Is there space for luggage?

    Yes, each passenger can carry 1 large bag (up to 25kg) and 1 hand luggage.

    24. Can I bring pets on the bus?

    Unfortunately, pets are not allowed for safety reasons.

    25. Are there rest stops during long trips?

    Yes, scheduled rest stops are included for longer routes like Addis Ababa – Gondar.

    Safety & Support

    26. Is it safe to travel with Ethio Guzo?

    Absolutely. Our drivers are trained, and buses are regularly maintained for safety.

    27. What if I lose something on the bus?

    Contact our support team as soon as possible with the bus number and your seat details.

    28. How can I contact support?

    Visit our Contact Us page or call our hotline at +251-900-000-000.

    29. Do you operate 24/7?

    Bookings are 24/7 online, but bus services run based on scheduled times. Support is available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

    30. Where are your offices located?

    We have main offices in Gondar, Bahir Dar, and Addis Ababa.

    About Ethio Guzo

    Ethio Guzo is a trusted and affordable bus transportation service connecting Gondar, Bahir Dar, and Addis Ababa. We make travel smooth, reliable, and safe.
